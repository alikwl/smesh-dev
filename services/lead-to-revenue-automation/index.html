Service FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI lead scoring actually work without hallucinating?

We feed raw lead form data (company size, budget, description) to OpenAI or Gemini APIs using a strict JSON-Schema response constraint. The model evaluates the lead against your historical customer parameters and returns a numerical score (1-100) and logical reasons, preventing any free-form hallucination.

Can it integrate with my existing WhatsApp Business and calendar?

Yes. Using n8n workflows, we connect your lead forms directly to the WhatsApp Business Cloud API and scheduling APIs (like Calendly or Cal.com), automating calendar updates and sending WhatsApp follow-ups.

What happens when a lead needs human follow-up?

When a lead qualifies (e.g. scores above 75), n8n triggers an instant notification with rich context to your team Slack channel or WhatsApp. It includes quick buttons to assign the lead, log notes, or trigger custom templates.

How quickly can I see results after launch?

Immediately. Once active, lead response times drop to under 3 seconds. Many clients report an immediate lift in sales bookings and a reduction in administrative pipeline friction within the first 14 days.

Do I own the entire CRM data and workflows?

Yes, 100%. We hand over all n8n workflows and the Supabase database. You retain full data privacy, compliance, and complete ownership with no platform vendor lock-in.
