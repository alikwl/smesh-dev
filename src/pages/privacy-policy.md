# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

At **Smesh.dev** (operated by **BismillahTC Digital Services**, based in Karachi, Pakistan), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, process, secure, and retain your data when you visit our website, download our developer blueprints, or interact with our consulting services.

## 1. Information We Collect
We only collect data that you voluntarily provide to us through our contact forms, newsletter subscription fields, resource gates, or floating AI support widgets. This information includes:
- **Identity Data:** Full name, company name, and business type.
- **Contact Data:** Email address, phone number, and WhatsApp number.
- **Project Details:** Operational bottlenecks, custom project descriptions, and estimated budgets.
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, resource download logs, and chat message transcripts.

## 2. How We Store and Process Your Data
To ensure high performance and reliability, Smesh.dev uses a modern, automated database infrastructure:
- **Supabase Database:** Form submissions and lead details are stored securely in our central PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase (using standard server encryption and Row Level Security).
- **n8n Workflows:** Submissions trigger automated n8n workflows that route leads, sync details to Google Sheets, and notify our team. Your data is not cached on intermediary workflow servers.

## 3. Data Sharing and Retention
We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We only share details with authorized tools (e.g., Supabase, WhatsApp APIs) to process your direct requests. We retain your contact information as long as required to deliver our consulting services or until you request its deletion.

## 4. Your Rights and Deletion Requests
Under governing regulations, you have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal details stored in our databases. To request data removal, please contact us:
