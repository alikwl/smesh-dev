    <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold font-display text-slate-900 mt-8">1. What are Cookies?</h2>
    <p>Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.</p>

    <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold font-display text-slate-900 mt-8">2. What Cookies Do We Use?</h2>
    <p>We use very few cookies. They fall into the following categories:</p>
    <ul class="list-disc pl-6 space-y-2">
      <li><strong>Essential Cookies:</strong> These are cookies required for technical operations, such as keeping track of session IDs or maintaining widget configurations (e.g. if you open or close the floating chat badge).</li>
      <li><strong>Analytics Cookies:</strong> Optional web analytics cookies (such as Google Analytics) to help us measure site performance and layout response rates.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold font-display text-slate-900 mt-8">3. Controlling Cookies</h2>
    <p>Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. If you block essential cookies, some interactive features (such as the instant webhook-linked chat widget) may not function as intended.</p>
