Running a local shop or clothing store in Pakistan means managing a constant stream of messages on WhatsApp. For many business owners, WhatsApp is their main sales channel. But when you receive dozens of inquiries at once, customers end up waiting, and you lose sales.

In this post, we explain how WhatsApp bots and workflow automations can turn your business number into an automated sales machine.

### The Problem: Manual Reply Bottlenecks

Usually, a customer sends a message asking for product pictures, sizes, and prices. A staff member has to:
1. Search through their phone gallery for pictures.
2. Check the store shelf to see if the item is in stock.
3. Calculate prices and delivery fees manually.
4. Keep track of customer names, shipping addresses, and payment receipts.

This manual process takes 10 to 15 minutes per customer. During busy seasons, it becomes impossible to manage, leading to missed opportunities.

### The Solution: Automated Order Systems

By integrating the Official WhatsApp Business Cloud API with workflow engines like n8n, you can automate almost the entire process:

- **Instant Product Buttons:** Customers can click custom buttons like "Browse Lawn Collection" or "Track Order" and receive immediate replies.
- **Google Sheets Stock Checks:** The automation checks your database spreadsheet instantly to see if a product is available, ensuring you never sell out-of-stock items.
- **Automatic Calculations:** The bot calculates the total bill and shipping costs based on the city, and displays a clean order summary.
- **Fulfillment Alerts:** The system writes the order into a shipping sheet and sends a notification to your packaging team.
