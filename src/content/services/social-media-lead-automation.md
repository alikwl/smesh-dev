## The Real Cost of Slow Social Media Responses

When someone clicks your ad and fills out a form or sends a DM, they are in buying mode right now.

Most businesses take 30 minutes to several hours to reply. By that time:
* **The lead** has already contacted 2–3 competitors.
* **Interest** has dropped significantly.
* **Cost per qualified lead** increases.

Studies consistently show that responding within 5 minutes can increase conversion rates by **3–10x** compared to responding after 30 minutes.

Social media lead automation solves the speed problem while keeping quality high.

---

## Complete System Flow

Here’s how a typical social media lead automation system works:

1. **Lead Comes In** — From Facebook/Instagram Lead Form, Instagram DM, LinkedIn message, or TikTok.
2. **Instant Auto-Reply** — Personalized message sent within seconds (e.g., “Thanks for your interest! Quick question to help you better…”).
3. **Smart Qualification** — The system asks 2–4 targeted questions to understand budget, timeline, and fit.
4. **Lead Scoring & Filtering** — Low-quality or mismatched leads get polite automated responses. High-intent leads are flagged.
5. **Smart Routing** — Qualified leads are automatically sent to WhatsApp (with full context) or pushed into your CRM with a notification to the right team member.
6. **Follow-up Sequences** — Automated reminders for leads that go cold.
7. **Dashboard & Analytics** — Track response time, qualification rate, and cost per qualified lead in real time.
